"The goal was never to replace a cardiologist. It was to give people a reason to go see one before it's too late."

Most ML health tools are black boxes that produce a number with no explanation. PulsePrism is different. It was built with explainability as a first principle — because in healthcare, understanding the reasoning matters as much as the result.

Trained with recall-optimised tuning, the model is designed to catch real risks rather than minimise false alarms — the right trade-off for a screening tool.
